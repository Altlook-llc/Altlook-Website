Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Altlook LLC ("we," "us," or "our") sets forth this Privacy Policy ("Policy") regarding the handling of user personal information in the mobile application "TaskEater" ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information.

  • Task data you enter or create in the App (task names, due dates, notes, etc.)
  • Device information (OS version, device model, app version, etc.)
  • Anonymous analytics data related to crashes and usage

The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number without your input.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes.

  • Providing App features (saving, displaying, and syncing tasks)
  • Improving App quality and fixing bugs
  • Usage analysis (as anonymized statistical data)
  • Responding to inquiries

3. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share user information with third parties except in the following cases.

  • When the user has given consent
  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect life, body, or property

4. External Services

The App may use third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, and similar purposes (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics). These providers process information according to their own privacy policies.

* Update this Policy once the external services in use are finalized.

5. Data Storage and Deletion

Task data is generally stored on your device. You can delete on-device data by uninstalling the App.

If you use cloud sync, data may be stored on servers operated by us or our contractors.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy as needed. The revised Policy takes effect when posted on this page.
